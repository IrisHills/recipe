Chai Tea Muffins

Enjoy the flavorful spices of chai tea in a delicious muffin!

Ingredients

½ teaspoon cinnamon

¼ teaspoon ground cardamom

¼ teaspoon ground cloves

¼ teaspoon ground allspice

¼ teaspoon of ginger

¼ cup chilled earl grey tea

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup milk

2 eggs

1 cup brown sugar

½ cup salted butter

2 teaspoons baking powder

2 cups flour

Directions

1.) Preheat oven to 350 f

2.) Line muffin cups with papers or coat pan in butter

3.) Cream butter and sugar together until smooth.

4.) Add eggs and vanilla to butter-sugar mixture and set aside

5.) Mix flour, baking powder, and salt into another bowl then set aside

6.) Combine the two mixtures together

7.) Pour mixture evenly in muffin cups

8.) Sprinkle brown sugar and cinnamon on top

9.) Bake for 20 minutes

10.)Let cool in pan for about 10 minutes and enjoy
